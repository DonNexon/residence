About Us

Residence Le Palmier is your perfect choice for luxurious and affordable apartment stays in Kigali, Rwanda. We offer modern, fully-furnished apartments in a peaceful and secure neighborhood. Our mission is to provide guests with top-tier comfort, hospitality, and a memorable experience.

Located at 6 KK 360 St, Kigali, our apartments are ideal for both short and long-term stays. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, our 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom options cater to all needs with excellent service and serene surroundings.

Choose Residence Le Palmier — your home away from home.
